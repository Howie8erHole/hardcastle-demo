How Hardcastle Works

🔔
1. Breach Detected

Hardcastle monitors your vault 24/7 and sends a system notification the moment any credentials appear in a known data breach.

🤖
2. AI Takes Action

One click. Hardcastle's AI opens its built-in sandboxed browser, navigates to the compromised site, logs in, and changes your password automatically.

✅
3. Vault Updated

Your vault updates instantly. The next time you visit that site, autofill works seamlessly with the new, secure password.

🧠
4. You Stay in Control

CAPTCHAs pause the AI and hand control to you. In Supervised Mode, you approve every change. You always have the final say.

Auto Mode
Recommended · Active

Hardcastle prepares everything and navigates to the password change page, but pauses and asks for your approval before submitting any change on any site. You stay in full control of every action.
