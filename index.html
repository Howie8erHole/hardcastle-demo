AI Remediation Mode
Control how Hardcastle handles password changes on your behalf
Auto Mode
Recommended · Active
Hardcastle handles everything automatically. It opens the affected site in its secure in-app browser, changes the password, and updates your vault — without you lifting a finger. Only pauses if a CAPTCHA requires human input.
Supervised Mode
Manual Approval
Hardcastle prepares everything and navigates to the password change page, but pauses and asks for your approval before submitting any change on any site. You stay in full control of every action.
